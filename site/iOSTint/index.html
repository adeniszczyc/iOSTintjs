iOSTint.js

A jQuery plugin to change the Safari URL bar tint colour on iOS7.

  1. Download the file from here, and include it, along with jQuery, at the bottom of your HTML. <script src="http://code.jquery.com/jquery-1.10.2.min.js"></script> <script src="js/iosTint.min.js"></script>
  2. Call the script beneath the include. <script> $.iosTint(color); </script> You can replace color with any valid html colour such as #69BEE5 , rgb(144,248,67) or even tomato .
